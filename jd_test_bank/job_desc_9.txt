About the job

Internship Type: Unpaid Internship


Position Overview

We are looking for a motivated Computer Science Intern with an interest in AI tools, automation, and “vibe coding” workflows to join our team in our New Jersey office. This is a hands-on opportunity for students or recent graduates who want real-world exposure to AI-assisted development, workflow automation, and software support projects.

The ideal candidate is curious, tech-savvy, eager to learn, and comfortable experimenting with modern AI tools such as ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, Claude, Gemini, or similar platforms.


Responsibilities

    Assist with AI-assisted coding and automation tasks.
    Support internal software and workflow improvement projects.
    Use AI tools to help generate, debug, and optimize code.
    Research and test new AI productivity tools and integrations.
    Collaborate with team members on small development projects.
    Help document workflows, prompts, and technical processes.
    Perform basic troubleshooting and technical support tasks when needed.


Requirements

    Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering, or related field.
    Basic understanding of programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, Java, or C++.
    Familiarity with AI coding assistants or generative AI tools.
    Good problem-solving and communication skills.
    Eagerness to learn new technologies.
    Ability to work onsite in our New Jersey office.


Preferred Skills

    Exposure to APIs, automation tools, or scripting.
    Basic understanding of machine learning or AI concepts.
    Familiarity with GitHub or version control.
    Experience building personal, academic, or freelance projects.


What You Will Gain

    Hands-on experience working with AI-powered development workflows.
    Exposure to real-world business and technical operations.
    Mentorship and practical learning opportunities.
    Opportunity to build experience for future software or AI-related roles.
