About the job

This position is based onsite in Parsippany, NJ. Relocation assistance is not available for this role.

Main Function

    Create, validate, implement and maintain PNY's software and hardware test methods
    Provide test engineering support for all PNY facilities

Qualifications

    Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or equivalent
    Advanced degree and/or additional technical education is a plus
    2+ years’ experience in a test engineering environment
    Excellent troubleshooting, problem solving and project management skills
    Ability to multitask, in a fast paced production environment
    Proficient with operating systems and component test hardware
    Working knowledge of :
        Automated Test Equipment (ATE)
        UNIX/Linux
        Programming languages a plus
    Familiar with the operations of memory, video and NAND Flash
    Excellent oral and written communications skills, including ability to communicate effectively in English
    Ability to travel (less than 5%)

Working Conditions

Time is split between regular office, lab and production test areas, with ESD procedures to be observed.

Key Relationships

Interfaces with Engineering, Manufacturing, Quality, Purchasing, Sales, Operations, suppliers and customers

Duties And Responsibilities

To perform this job successfully, the individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

    Create, validate and implement PNY's production software and hardware test approaches and methodologies
    Generate product application test reports (ATR) for new product at time of qualification
    Maintain PNY's manufacturing test platform software and hardware, including the test network
    Provide technical assistance and training to test lab staff
    Generate and validate production test software and hardware
    Perform system administration on all production test platforms at NJ location
    Coordinate with external agencies to ensure qualification of PNY products
    Coordinate with customers regarding test issues
    Assist in other areas, as assigned

Compensation

The base salary range for this position is $80,000.00 to $120,000.00; the exact salary depends on several factors such as experience, skills, education, and budget.
